## Key Model-Conditioning Takeaways

1. **Great Rotation means tech dip-buying unreliable** — mega-cap pullbacks deeper and slower than norms; Industrials/Energy/Staples pullbacks shallower
2. **Binary event density at multi-year high** — pharma patent cliff, Google antitrust, Boeing certs, CCCA, Basel III, FDX spinoff, UPS Teamsters
3. **Rate sensitivity varies enormously within sectors** — BAC most rate-sensitive bank, NEE most rate-sensitive utility, CHTR most rate-sensitive cable
4. **Earnings predictability = key feature** — VOLATILE stocks (TSLA, INTC, PLTR, ORCL, NKE, CVS, COF) need wider stops and longer recovery windows vs HIGH stocks (JNJ, KO, TXN, V, LMT)
