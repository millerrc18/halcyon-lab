## VIX Regime Framework

- **VIX 9-14 (Extreme complacency):** Pullbacks 1-3%, recover in days. But builds structural fragility — ends violently (2017→Volmageddon, 2024→yen unwind). Be most aggressive buying dips, hard stop when VIX breaks 30.
- **VIX 14-20 (Normal/healthy):** Pullbacks 3-7%, recover 2-4 weeks. Goldilocks for pullback strategies.
- **VIX 20-35 (Elevated grinding stress):** MOST DANGEROUS for pullback traders. No capitulation flush to buy. Reduce/avoid pullback buying when VIX averages >25 for 5+ consecutive days without spiking >40.
- **VIX >40 (Panic/capitulation):** VIX >60 = 100% reliable buy signal in this dataset (Volmageddon, COVID, Aug 2024, Apr 2025). VIX 40-60 = wait for confirmation.

## Transition Signals (Reliability Ranked)

**Highest:** Credit spread widening (HY OAS +50bp), VIX term structure inversion, breadth deterioration
**High:** Yield curve dynamics (2s10s <20bp), 10Y >4.5%, bonds selling during equity decline
**Medium:** Fed rhetoric shifts, positioning extremes (AAII, options), fund flows

## Failed Dip-Buying Regimes (4 critical periods)

1. **Q4 2018:** Lower highs/lower lows for 3 months, each bounce sold
2. **COVID Feb-Mar 2020:** +9.3% bounce Mar 13 → -12% crash Mar 16
3. **2022 Bear (Jan-Oct):** 9 months of "peak inflation" rallies reversing to new lows
4. **Liberation Day (Feb-Apr 2025):** Buying -10% correction suffered another -10%

Common precursors: Credit spreads widening + VIX elevated without capitulation + breadth deterioration
