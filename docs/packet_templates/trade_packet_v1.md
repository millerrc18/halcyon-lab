# Trade Packet Template v1

## Email subject examples
- `[TRADE DESK] Morning Watchlist - 2026-03-22`
- `[TRADE DESK] Action Packet - AAPL`
- `[TRADE DESK] EOD Recap - 2026-03-22`

## Packet design standard
Top section should be a quick bullet brief that can be read in under 30 seconds.
Bottom section should provide deeper support, context, and defensibility.

---

# Quick Bullet Brief

- **Ticker / Company:**
- **Recommendation:** Buy / Watch / Pass
- **Setup Type:** Pullback in strong trend / relative strength continuation
- **Priority:** High / Medium / Low
- **Why now:**
- **Entry Zone:**
- **Stop / Invalidation:**
- **Targets:**
- **Expected Hold Period:** Typically 2 to 10 trading days
- **Suggested Position Size:** $ allocation, % of capital, est. $ risk to stop
- **Event Risk:** Normal / Earnings in X days / Other catalyst
- **Confidence:** 1 to 10

# Deeper Analysis

## Thesis
- What changed
- Why this is attractive now
- Why this name instead of alternatives

## Structure and Context
- Trend summary
- Relative strength summary
- Pullback quality
- Broad market context
- Sector / industry context

## Risk / Invalidation
- What would break the thesis
- What would make this a pass instead
- Reward / risk framing

## Monitoring Plan
- What to watch after entry
- What would increase conviction
- What would trigger exit or reduction

## Event-Risk Section (required if relevant)
- Next earnings date
- Does expected hold window overlap earnings?
- Gap-risk warning
- Does thesis assume exit before earnings or hold through event?
- Conservative sizing note

## Follow-up Readiness
The assistant should be prepared to answer:
- Why now?
- Why this ticker?
- Why not another candidate?
- What changed since the last scan?
- What would invalidate the idea?
